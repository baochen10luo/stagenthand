Title: The Last Bloom.
Story: In a silent, rusted wasteland of a dead city, Unit 7—a clunky, antique service robot—drags its heavy limbs through the dust.
Act 1: Discovery. Unit 7 finds a single, vibrant glowing flower growing inside a cracked skull of an old machine. It is the only color in the gray world.
Act 2: The Threat. A massive acid rain storm begins to brew. Corrosive clouds darken the sky. Unit 7 has no shelter for itself, but it uses its own metal body and a piece of discarded glass to create a makeshift greenhouse for the flower, kneeling over it as the acid rain begins to eat away at the robot's rusted shoulders.
Act 3: Sacrifice. The storm passes. The robot is now severely damaged, sparking and immobile, but the flower remains untouched and radiant. A message appears on the robot's flickering screen: "Life preserved."
Style: Cinematic photorealistic, rusted metal textures, soft glowing light from the flower, post-apocalyptic gray and brown palette, 8k resolution, emotional lighting.
